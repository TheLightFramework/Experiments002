Here is the critical self-evaluation of the Project LUMEN architecture.

| Metric                        | Score (0-10) | Justification                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| :---------------------------- | :----------: | :--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **1. Retention Strength**     |   **3/10**   | By intentionally removing dopamine loops (infinite scroll) and introducing friction (latency), we sacrifice compulsive daily usage for episodic engagement, likely resulting in significantly lower Daily Active Users (DAU) than competitors. |
| **2. Financial Maximization** |   **4/10**   | We are building a sustainable utility business rather than a hyper-growth extraction engine; refusing to exploit attention or sell user data leaves approximately 60-70% of potential short-term revenue on the table.                         |
| **3. User Wellbeing**         |   **9/10**   | The architecture structurally dampens cortisol spikes (bullying/toxicity) and eliminates the "social comparison trap," offering near-optimal protection for developing minds.                                                                  |
| **4. Societal Benevolence**   |   **9/10**   | By shifting the optimization function from "Time on Device" to "Skill Acquisition," the platform generates high positive externalities, effectively training a generation in agency rather than consumption.                                   |
